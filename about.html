Founder · Consultus AI

Chris Holland

Before founding Consultus AI, I ran data for the fastest-growing pet insurance company in North America — leading the team from early scale through a private equity exit five years later.

That run taught me what actually moves the needle inside a high-growth business: clean pipelines, honest dashboards, testable decisions, and relentless conversion-rate work. The tools are well understood. What's rare is the discipline to wire them together correctly and the operator judgment to know what's worth measuring in the first place.

Consultus AI is how I bring that playbook to small businesses — the ones that can't hire an enterprise data team but deserve enterprise-quality answers. AI is the force multiplier that makes the economics work. A human (me) is on every engagement, every review, every reply.

How I work

A few things I care about.

Own it end-to-end

If a number on your dashboard is wrong, that's my problem. I don't hand off. I don't disappear. I ship fixes, not finger-pointing.

Work in public

Every transform is in your GitHub. Every dashboard is in your Metabase. No hidden models. No proprietary layer you'd lose if I walked away.

Explain the reasoning

If I'm making a modeling choice, you hear why. If an A/B result is ambiguous, I say so. Good data work is mostly being honest about what you don't know.
