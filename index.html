Our philosophy
It's not about the product.
It's about the outcome.

Tools are table stakes. Anyone can buy a warehouse and spin up a dashboard. The only question that matters is whether the numbers moved — whether the business runs better on Friday than it did on Monday. That's what we're selling. That's how we're judged.

What we do

Three pillars. One system.

Every engagement composes the same three primitives. The mix changes by industry, but the machinery stays the same — humans designing and reviewing, AI doing the heavy lifting.

01 / Data

Warehoused & modelled

Raw → cleaned → dashboard-ready. BigQuery and Dataform under the hood, every transformation in version control, every change a reviewable PR — drafted with AI, shipped by us.

02 / Automation

Workflows that run themselves

Delinquency chases. Quote submissions. Daily alerts. If it's repetitive and rule-bound, it's a candidate for automation — and we ship it.

03 / AI

Operator-facing intelligence

Ask your data questions in English. Get new dashboards on request. AI does the building; we review every change so nothing ships you wouldn't want shipped.
